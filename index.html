The City of Waterfalls

Bangon Falls

The estimated height of Bangon Falls is 60 meters. You wouldn’t see where the water of Bangon Falls comes from since it stretches very far.

It has slippery parts due to moss and a pond at the top where people can enjoy swimming because the water is deep enough. It is so tall that you can even trek up to the top!

Malajog Zipline

The thrill-seeking activities of the Nature Park and zipline ensure visitors can get a spectacular view of the open sea.

It only takes a minute to ride the zip line. There are other activities that people can join in, like swimming, going on a banana boat ride, and Jet Ski ride on Lo-oc Beach, and a river cruise at Bolongto River.

Tarangban Falls

These two falls can satisfy your cravings for a good swimming and chilling spot. The entrance fee for Busay Falls is free while you need a guide going to Tarangban Falls.

Both Falls need to be reached through trekking, and both are unique in their own way and filled with healthy trees and magnificent rock formations.
